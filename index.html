We know what you're thinking...

"Salmon cookies?? Is this a joke?"

Well, yes... and no. Technically, our cookies started as a joke between Patrick "Pat" Eli Krahenbuhl and a few Seattle friends. But Pat was inspired by the wild success of the savory-to-sweet crossover that bacon had achieved atop maple donuts and decided that salmon deserved a fair shot at confectionery glory, too!

Pat got to work and quickly discovered that just a small piece of smoked salmon could infuse an entire double batch of maple cookie with a sublime flavor that surpassed all his expectations. It took some convincing to get his friends and family to try his unconventional creation, but invariably they were all converted to diehard salmon cookie fanatics. They began begging Pat to make his unusual cookies for parties, weddings, and funerals. As the word spread, the demand for these intriguing delights grew so strong that Pat had no choice but to set up shop and make them available to the world!
